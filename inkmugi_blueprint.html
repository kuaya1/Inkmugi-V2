BUTTON RULES
1. Max 2 primary CTAs visible at once (hero + sticky mobile). Everything else is ghost or secondary.
2. Radius = 2px always. Never pill. Never squared (0px reads brutalist). 2px is the system signature.

§E The Ink Mugi Method

§E — proprietary method — 3 steps
OUR METHOD — TRADEMARKED

Three phases. No shortcuts.

Every client, every time. This is the protocol behind a 0.19% complication rate.

01

Mapping

40-minute consultation. Facial symmetry analysis, bone structure mapping, skin-type pigment matching. You approve the drawn shape before a single needle touches your skin. No drawing — no procedure.

02

Pigment match

Medical-grade Permablend pigments, matched to your natural brow tone + skin undertone. Patch-tested if you request. We mix your exact formula in-session — never pre-mixed, never reused.

03

Layered shading

Three pigment passes, building density gradually. Topical anesthetic throughout. 90-minute session. You watch every stroke in a handheld mirror and direct final symmetry yourself.
